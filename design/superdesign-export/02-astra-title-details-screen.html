The Story

In a world where hunters, humans who possess magical abilities, must battle deadly monsters to protect the human race from certain annihilation, a notoriously weak hunter named Sung Jin-woo finds himself in a seemingly endless struggle for survival.

After narrowly surviving an overwhelming powerful double dungeon that nearly wiped out his entire party, a mysterious program called the System selects him as its sole player and in turn, gives him the extremely rare ability to level up in strength, possibly beyond any known limits.
